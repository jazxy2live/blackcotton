# 📚 Reading List — Prioritized

Read these in this order. Papers 1-4 are must-reads, rest are supporting.

## 🔴 MUST READ

### 1. Chinese Melanin-in-Cotton (Most relevant to us)
**"Brown cotton fibre produced by expression of tyrosinase gene from Streptomyces antibioticus"**
- Xu et al.
- Used dORF438 + dTYRA from S. antibioticus in cotton cv. Xinluzao 6
- ISAAA: https://www.isaaa.org/kc/cropbiotechupdate/article/default.asp?ID=15978
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ (search: "tyrosinase cotton fiber melanin Streptomyces")

### 2. MYB113 Brown Fiber (Best genetics paper)
**"The transcription factor MYB113 governs anthocyanin biosynthesis and brown fiber in cotton"**
- Wang N. et al. (2022)
- Journal: **Plant Physiology**, June 27, 2022
- https://academic.oup.com/plphys/article/189/3/1510/6618066
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35766874/

### 3. GhTT2 Temporal Brown Fiber (Validates timing strategy)
**"GhTT2-3A activates brown fiber color during SCW thickening with quality preserved"**
- Mapped Lc1 locus to chromosome A07, 1.4 Mb inversion upstream of TT2
- Proved timing-specific expression preserves fiber quality
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ (search: "GhTT2 Lc1 brown cotton fiber proanthocyanidin")

### 4. Betalain Pink Cotton (Quality data we can reference)
**"Engineering betalain biosynthesis in cotton for pink fibers"**
- CAAS Cotton Research Institute (2023)
- Journal: **Plant Biotechnology Journal**
- Fiber quality comparable to wild-type at 45 DPA
- Betalains faded due to vacuolar degradation
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ (search: "betalain cotton fiber pink CAAS")

## 🟡 IMPORTANT CONTEXT

### 5. Mega-QTL Analysis (2,864 QTLs mapped)
**"Meta-QTL analysis of fiber quality traits in cotton"**
- 2024, integrated 50 studies (2000-2024)
- Found 75 MQTLs, 1,035 linked to fiber quality
- Proves color-quality linkage is chromosomal
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ (search: "meta-QTL cotton fiber quality 2024")

### 6. GhMYB7 → CesA Regulation (Promoter mechanics)
**"GhMYB7 promotes secondary wall cellulose deposition by regulating GhCesA gene expression"**
- August 2021
- Shows HOW cellulose timing is controlled
- Useful for understanding what promoter elements to use
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ (search: "GhMYB7 CesA cotton cellulose")

### 7. Cotton Fiber Transcriptional Landscape
**"Transcriptional Landscape of Cotton Fiber Development"**
- Frontiers in Plant Science, February 2022
- Remapped 380+ RNA-seq datasets, 18 tissues, 34 cultivars
- Stage-specific cis-regulatory elements identified
- https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/ (search: "transcriptional landscape cotton fiber development 2022")

### 8. CSIRO Betalain Cotton (2024 peer-reviewed)
**"Expression of betalain pigment genes in cotton fibers"**
- CSIRO / Dr. Colleen MacMillan
- Genes: BvDODA1, BvCYP76AD1 (red beet), MjcDOPA5GT (four o'clock flower)
- Published via CSIRO Publications 2024
- https://publications.csiro.au/ (search: "betalain cotton fiber")

### 9. Daily Fiber Transcriptome (Best expression data)
**"High-resolution model of gene expression during G. hirsutum fiber development"**
- Daily transcriptomic changes from 6-24 DPA
- Identifies SCW synthesis genes day by day
- ResearchGate preprint
- Search: "high resolution cotton fiber transcriptome daily DPA"

## 🟢 BACKGROUND / REFERENCE

### 10. Naturally Colored Cotton Review (2024-2025)
**"Genetic and molecular basis of cotton fiber color"**
- Review covering anthocyanins, gossypol, and engineering strategies
- Published in Fiber and Textile Journal, March 2025
- https://fbtjournal.com/ (search: "cotton fiber color genetic molecular")

### 11. Cotton pGhFDH Promoter (2024)
**"Isolation and functional characterization of pGhFDH constitutive promoter"**
- February 2024
- Shows promoter isolation from cotton is feasible
- Strong in fibers at 5-10 DPA (elongation, not useful for us but method is)
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ (search: "pGhFDH promoter cotton 2024")

### 12. Bio-Indigo from E. coli (Competing approach)
**"Microbial factory for bio-indigo synthesis and sustainable cotton dyeing"**
- RSC journal, May 2025
- Engineering E. coli to produce bio-indigo
- Still a dye (goes ON fiber, not IN it) — will still fade
- https://pubs.rsc.org/ (search: "bio-indigo E. coli cotton 2025")

### 13. Saski / Clemson Cotton Award
**"Cotton transformation efficiency and naturally pigmented fibers"**
- Dr. Chris Saski, Clemson University
- 2025 Cotton Genetics Research Award
- >50% transformation efficiency (patented)
- Targeting blue hues
- https://www.clemson.edu/ (search: "Saski cotton genetics research award 2025")
